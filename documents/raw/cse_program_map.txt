OVERVIEW
This program map is an unofficial advising document for the UC Merced Computer Science and Engineering major. It is intended for course planning only and does not replace the official UC Merced Catalog or advisor approval.

IMPORTANT PREREQUISITE NOTE
CSE 100, MATH 024, MATH 032 and CSE 031 are prerequisites for most upper-division CSE courses. Some CSE courses may have additional requirements, so students should check the schedule of courses.

TECHNICAL ELECTIVE REQUIREMENT
CSE technical electives require 30 total units.
- 20 units must be CSE upper-division courses.
- 10 units may come from an approved list of courses.
- Courses do not double count.

BIO/ESS REQUIREMENT
Students choose one Bio/ESS course. Options listed on the map include:
- BIO 043
- BIO 003
- BIO 005
- BIO 047
- ESS 001
- ESS 005

The map recommends BIO 043 because it may help with Global Awareness and Sustainability badges.

GENERAL EDUCATION NOTES
Writing in the Discipline:
- The map recommends CSE 155, WRI 100, or ENGR 156.

AREA B: Social Science, Arts, and Humanities:
Students must take three courses in Social Science, Arts, and Humanities.
- At least one course must be from Social Science.
- At least one course must be from Arts and Humanities.
- Students should try to overlap these courses with Intellectual Experience badges.

INTELLECTUAL EXPERIENCE BADGES
One course can satisfy a maximum of two badges.

Suggested strategy:
- Scientific Method: completed with major requirements.
- Quantitative and Numerical Analysis: completed with major requirements.
- Ethics: completed with major requirements.
- Media and Visual Analysis: take CSE 160 as a technical elective.
- Global Awareness: take BIO 043.
- Sustainability: take BIO 043.
- Literary and Textual Analysis: take one class that overlaps with SSHA GE.
- Societies and Cultures of the Past: take one class that overlaps with SSHA GE.
- Diversity and Identity: take one class that overlaps with SSHA GE.
