Course-Specific Feedback:

----------------------------------

CSE 100: Algorithm Design and Analysis

Frequently Mentioned Strengths

Curves exams and final grades.
Open to student feedback.
Generally viewed as kind and approachable.

Frequently Mentioned Challenges

Exams account for approximately 80% of the course grade.
Lectures often follow slides closely.
Limited study materials for exams.
Students frequently report learning the material independently.

Representative Student Opinions

Course is difficult even with grading adjustments.
Success often requires extensive self-study.
Lectures may not provide sufficient preparation for exams.
Many students view CSE 100 as significantly harder because of course structure and assessment weighting.

----------------------------------

CSE 141: Introduction to Compiler Construction

Frequently Mentioned Strengths

Participation exercises may reinforce lecture material.
Some students report that attending class and participating helps exam preparation.

Frequently Mentioned Challenges

Lecture organization and clarity are recurring concerns.
Some students report difficulty understanding explanations.
Several reviews mention communication issues.

Representative Student Opinions

Students report mixed experiences.
Reviews range from positive participation-based learning experiences to significant dissatisfaction with lecture quality.

----------------------------------

CSE 165: Introduction to Object-Oriented Programming

Frequently Mentioned Strengths

Labs are generally considered manageable.
Some students describe the professor as flexible and willing to accommodate requests.
Feedback occasionally resulted in course adjustments.

Frequently Mentioned Challenges

Lectures frequently described as difficult to follow.
Project expectations are sometimes unclear.
Students report inconsistent communication regarding assignments and grading.
Labs and projects are often viewed as graded strictly.

Representative Student Opinions

Some students appreciated flexibility and responsiveness.
Others felt the course lacked clear instruction and organization.
Reviews are mixed but lean negative overall.

Recurring Themes Across All Courses

Teaching Style

Heavy reliance on lecture slides.
Students often report needing independent learning.
Lecture clarity is the most frequently mentioned concern.

Grading

Exams often carry significant weight.
Curves and grading adjustments are common.
Students have mixed opinions about fairness and consistency.

Course Difficulty

CSE 100 is frequently described as difficult regardless of instructor.
CSE 141 is considered technically challenging.
CSE 165 receives mixed difficulty ratings depending on prior programming experience.

Overall Student Consensus

Students generally view Professor Pengfei Su as approachable and willing to accommodate feedback, but many reviews express concerns regarding lecture effectiveness, communication, and heavy reliance on self-study. The strongest recurring themes are difficult exams, slide-based lectures, and substantial independent learning requirements.
