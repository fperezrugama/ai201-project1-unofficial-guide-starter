Content:

OVERVIEW
Students pursuing the B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering must complete all required courses with a C- or better.

REPEAT POLICY
- Students must repeat required courses if they receive:
  D+, D, D-, F, Unsatisfactory, or Not Passed.
- A course may normally be repeated only once.
- Additional attempts require petition approval.
